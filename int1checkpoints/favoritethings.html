Taiyaki


I also love Taiyaki! Taiyaki is a Japanese dessert that is kind of like two pancakes with slightly sweet red bean paste or cream in the middle. It's a very common street food and it's really good! Taiyaki is made in a special fish mold cooker. If you want to make your own authetic Tayaki, you can buy a taiyaki maker from Cool Stuff Japan , a really nice website for buying cool stuff from Japan!
